The AutoMMM Solution

AutoMMM introduces a novel, fully automated system using a team of specialized AI agents. It bridges the gap by providing rapid, data-driven insights to optimize marketing strategy without the need for manual intervention.

System Architecture: A Multi-Agent Team

AutoMMM operates as a coordinated team of AI agents, managed by a Supervisor. This "orchestrator-worker" design, built with LangChain and LangGraph, ensures a reliable and flexible workflow from data input to final recommendation.
